Family Picnic
18th August 2024, 12:00-17:00
Holburne Museum
A chance to sit, relax and listen to some live local music.
During Bath Pride we are holding a family picnic in the grounds of the Holburne Museum. We will be hosting local musicians to serenade you and charities and other organisations will be there so you can find out what they do and how you can help further LGBT+ rights in Bath.

What's on offer

We are still in the process of finalising some of the details to bring you a magical first Bath Pride. What we can say is that we will be having a family picnic in the grounds, so remember to bring your food and drinks with you, to relax and sit alongside the Bath Pride family. There is a cafe within the Holburne Museum if you are feeling peckish.
We will also have some local musicians that will be playing some music. We are so looking forward to announcing them in the run up to the event so keep an eye on our socials!
There will also be some charities and other organisations hosting stalls in the Holburne Museum grounds. Pride is more than just celebrating the LGBT+ community, but an opportunity to make Bath a more welcoming city, and we hope you will go along and talk to them about the work that they do.

Where to find us

The Holburne Museum is a ten minute walk from the centre of town along Great Pulteney Street and a 15 minute walk from Bath Spa Train Station (0.8miles). They have bike racks available as well.
The car park operates under the MiPermit scheme enabling you to pay via your mobile. Their location number is 701060. The car park machine, located by the entrance to the car park, also accepts coins. They have a pay & display car park and a number of free disabled parking spaces for Blue Badge holders.
